Crime in India

Opening up India's crime data

Crime data in India has always been buried in long PDF documents that are difficult to read and analyze. Crime in India makes India's crime records more meaningful and easy to use. With five years' worth of the most updated data from the National Crime Records Bureau — 2010 to 2014 — you can now access information that governs your lives.

Know your city and its crime history. Track different kinds of crimes.

Crimes

Choose a category of crime

'Violent crimes' include crimes like murder, attempt to commit murder, riots. 'Crimes against women' include rape, sexual harassment and molestation, dowry deaths. Burglary, arson, car thefts are among those classified as 'property crimes'. Economic crimes include, among others, cheating and forgery.
